SEO helps your business rank on Google, thereby getting your business in front of your target audience. It increases awareness about your business, its services and helps draw in traffic to your website. It is from this traffic that conversion and sales happen. Without SEO, your site has very low chances of ranking on the first page of Google.
Here are some main advantages –
  1. SEO builds trust and credibility for your business. Your customer trusts you and is willing to buy from you.
  2. It helps increase your brand visibility, helping you reach a wide set of audience, who would otherwise not know that your business existed. SEO helps them access your business and that means leads for you.
  3. It helps you beat competition.
  4. You get organic traffic. This means, if you rank on Google, you get a steady stream of traffic for which you do not pay per click (which is the case when you run PPC ads).
Should you invest in SEO? Yes you must. SEO is part of digital marketing that is based on intent and data. The benefits of SEO are enormous if done right. Since its your biggest tool to get traffic and leads, it should not be an integral part of your marketing strategy

SEO is a complex and an ever-changing process. You will need a team of experts (like an SEO strategist, On-page and Off-page SEO executives, and content marketers) who understand the processes and trade which is required to rank high on Google SERPs, to make businesses searchable, and to drive relevant traffic. It requires constant effort (month after month), diligent research, smart strategies to break into Page 1 of search and retain the rankings. So, whether you need to hire an SEO agency or not depends on few factors. For example:
  • Do you already have people in your team who know about SEO?
  • Do you have time and resources to allocate for SEO, month and month?
  • Can you handle a multi-channel SEO campaign?
  • Can you afford to develop SEO competencies in the current stage of business development
You are best suited to hire an SEO agency if you do not want to engage in the above mentioned activities.

It’s important to keep in mind that SEO is a process that yields results in the medium and long-term. You cannot expect to launch your website and expect it to rank it 3 months. To perform organically, a website needs to build authority. While ranking for local keywords or long-tail keywords may take shorter time (around 3-6 months), ranking for short tail keywords which has huge competition can take much longer (more than 12 months even). Ranking also depends on technical issues like how light is your website, how fast does it open on mobile, how easy the navigation is for the user, how great the design is. If all works fine, you should start an uptick in traffic within 3 months itself. Most clients start seeing substantial results anywhere between 12 to 20 weeks, if all on-page issues have been resolved.

Finalizing the best SEO agency for your business can be a bit of a challenge, considering there are hundreds and thousands of agencies offering identical services. However, here are a list of tips that could help you finalize the most relevant & best SEO agency for your business:
  • Invest time while researching for SEO agency suited to your business requirements
  • Evaluate client reviews, testimonials, third-party rating, success stories, and awards & recognition
  • Ensure transparency and simplicity in pricing
  • Check if they have industry expertise which you are looking for
  • Check if they can provide you with custom SEO strategies to outrank competition
  • Check if the agency provides time-bound performance reports
  • Check if the agency can provide you with timelines for deliverables
  • Ensure that the agency you are going to hire is easy to reach and have quick turn-around time.

While there are a number ways to increase visibility for your business on search engines, one of the best ways is by organically appearing on search. This can happen when your website has been optimised for the search engines and SEO is about all those techniques that makes this happen.

When your website starts ranking on Google for keywords that are relevant for your customers, you are basically making the customer aware of your brand, or re-enforcing your brand’s existence. When a customer sees your brand appear on top searches for his queries, he starts acknowledging your brand’s existence and starts trusting it.

The work of SEO is to get your brand in front of your customer, by making it rank for the relevant keywords. At Justwords, our SEO experts usually offer a free SEO strategy session, where we discuss with our clients the best moves to boost traffic and rankings.

Hence, if you are looking to increase your business’ visibility on Google, the first step would be to invest in SEO and then get the right team to work for your brand.

There is a common perception that SEO is complex and generally cannot be understood. We are not saying it’s not complex, but if done right, it should lead to results, in should show in the metrics of traffic, leads, conversions etc. So how do you know whether your SEO is working?

Well one of the most immediate signs that your SEO is working is an increase in organic traffic to the website, and an upward movement of ranking of certain keywords on the SERPs. That is the most direct sign that your SEO campaign is in the right direction. Also your CTR should also improve since your website is now showing up on searches and getting relevant traffic. This is what is getting you more traffic.

Also, you can always ask your SEO agency or SEO team member to share the metrics with you and explain to what exactly is this happening. A good agency will explain the picture very clearly and also tell you what the future path looks like, or at least the strategy they are working on to improve SEO results.
